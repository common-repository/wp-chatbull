== Description ==
Now chat with your website visitors with WP ChatBull. This is a perfect fit for Small Business for both who sell products and services. It hosts data & files completely on your own server. No need to connect with any third party or pay subscriptions. Install WP ChatBull today to find your potential customers and improve your conversion rate.
 
You can upgrade to [ChatBull Pro](https://codecanyon.net/item/support-chat-php-live-chat-with-desktop-and-mobile-app/15044181 "ChatBull Pro") to have Desktop (for both Widnows & Mac) and Android apps for operators.
